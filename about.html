About

Built from a lifelong drive to create.

Seventh Boar Development is an independent Australian studio shaped by a decade of learning, experimenting, and building whenever time allowed, from early game prototypes to apps, websites, and polished digital products.

Games with feeling. Apps with purpose. Built with bite.

Origin Games came first

The studio began with the childhood dream of making games and the drive to turn ideas into playable worlds.

Discipline Built around service

Years in the Army shaped the way the work is approached: steady, practical, and committed to finishing properly.

Direction Apps, games, and beyond

Seventh Boar now builds useful apps, engaging games, and digital products with a clear sense of purpose.

The Story

Seventh Boar Development started with the same dream many kids grow up with: wanting to make games. That curiosity turned into action after buying a first proper computer and spending free time in Unity, building small projects, testing ideas, and learning by doing.

The path into development was not a straight line. After school, with the games industry still feeling difficult to reach, the next chapter became the Army. Even there, programming never stopped. Every spare chance became time to keep learning, moving from Unity into Unreal Engine, Godot, mobile apps, and anything else that could sharpen the craft.

After a spinal injury brought that military chapter to a close, the focus became clearer: turn years of on-and-off building into something real. Seventh Boar Development is the result of that persistence, combining roughly a decade of learning with the goal of building apps that help people and games that bring back the feeling of the experiences we grew up with.

What Drives The Studio
  • Apps that are useful, calm, and built around real daily life.
  • Games that chase strong atmosphere, satisfying play, and memorable feeling.
  • Websites and digital products that present ideas with clarity and polish.
  • A constant willingness to learn new tools, engines, platforms, and workflows.
Studio Approach

Seventh Boar Development favours clean design, considered features, and products that feel straightforward on the surface even when the build underneath is complex. The studio is built on persistence, self-learning, and the belief that good products come from caring about how people actually use them.

Experience

The work draws from time spent across Unity, Unreal Engine, Godot, mobile app development, websites, and cross-platform product builds. That range gives Seventh Boar a practical, flexible base for building games, apps, and everything in between.
