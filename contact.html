What Helps In A First Message
  • What the project is and who it is for.
  • What already exists and what still feels unresolved.
  • The kind of help you think would make the most difference right now.
Simple Brief Template
  1. 01
    The build Name the game, app, or product and what it is supposed to do.
  2. 02
    The problem Explain what currently feels weak, unclear, unfinished, or risky.
  3. 03
    The target Describe what “better” would look like if this collaboration went well.
Typical Scope
  • A short polish sprint around a specific feature, flow, or system.
  • A mid-length collaboration to restructure a prototype or product experience.
  • Launch-adjacent support for presentation, UX, and narrative clarity.
Why The Filler Matters
