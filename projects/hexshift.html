Why include prototypes on the site

Prototype pages show how the studio thinks before a project becomes final. They give visitors evidence of experimentation, taste, and systems reasoning, which is often as persuasive as a finished release.

This kind of page is especially useful for attracting collaborators who care about problem-solving and iteration quality, not just polish at the end.

Great prototype case study ingredients

  • What question the prototype was trying to answer.
  • What changed after testing or play sessions.
  • What insights carried forward into later work.

Project Value

  • ClarityShow the reason behind the experiment.
  • RestraintKeep scope tight and the insights visible.
  • PresentationTreat R&D work with the same care as shipped work.
