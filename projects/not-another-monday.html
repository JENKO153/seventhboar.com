The product story

For supportive or routine-based products, the interface has to do more than look clean. It needs to reduce hesitation, guide the user naturally, and keep progress feeling visible without becoming noisy.

This page template is built to show exactly how those decisions were made, from the onboarding flow to the framing of goals, check-ins, and progress.
