Case Study Anatomy

Each project should tell a full story.

The best layout for this site is one where portfolio cards promise more than visuals. They should imply there is real thinking waiting underneath: what changed, what got refined, and why the final version feels better than the first idea.

  • Context that explains what the project was trying to do.
  • Process notes that show the decisions, not just the deliverables.
  • Outcome framing that helps the work feel useful, not ornamental.
